Sound Design for the Marching Arts

Modern audio design for marching band, indoor percussion, winds, and color guard.

I provide professional audio services for the marching arts, specializing in marching band, indoor percussion, indoor winds, and color guard. My work includes audio consultation, custom sound design, synth enhancement, and audio system installation.

As electronics continue to play a larger role in modern productions, ensembles need reliable and well designed audio systems. I focus on delivering stable, practical, and polished solutions that enhance performance on the field and floor.

About

Who I am

My name is Jayden Brown. After discovering my passion for the marching arts in 2010 alongside my interest in the AV industry, I have focused on bringing those two worlds together. I am passionate about helping ensembles enhance their productions through thoughtful and reliable audio design.

As an audio and video professional working with venues throughout the region, I support events that serve hundreds of thousands of guests each year. Over the past 15 years, my goal has been to deliver dependable service to every client, whether that means audio consultation, live mixing support, or designing full soundscapes for productions. If you are interested in improving the audio experience for your ensemble, get in touch and we can discuss how I can help your organization.
